SVGOMG is a tool that I frequently use and got to know thourgh the Simple Icon project. I myself and the Simple Icons community were facing an issue regarding the settings of the tool, and wanted a way to reset them. Hence, I went ahead an proposed a reset button in Pull Request #141, which lead to Pull Request #142 in which I collaborated with Jake Archibald (the maintainer of SVGOMG) to get a working reset button for the settings on the website.

You can now use the button on the site! It is at the bottom of the settings panel.
